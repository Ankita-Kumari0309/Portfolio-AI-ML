About Me

Turning data into
deployable intelligence

I'm a AI & Machine Learning Engineer currently pursuing my Master of Computer Applications at BIT Mesra, with a sharp focus on building AI systems that go beyond notebooks and into production environments.

My expertise spans NLP pipelines, Generative AI applications, multi-agent architectures, and RAG-based systems. I'm particularly drawn to the challenge of making LLMs reliable, verifiable, and grounded in real-world data.

I've built systems at CSIR (sign language recognition using MMPose + PyTorch, ~92% accuracy) and AICTE/Edunet (movie recommendation on 5000+ TMDb titles), and independently shipped three Azure-deployed AI projects including a News Intelligence System and a Multi-Agent IT Ticket Resolver.

Beyond code — I enjoy chess, writing articles, and reading. I believe great ML engineers are curious learners first.
